**Measurement** (how it will be tested), **Status** (Proposed / Testing /
Confirmed / Refuted), **Result**. A confirmed hypothesis drops into the relevant
`Lessons/` file as a `## entry` and shrinks to one line here; a refuted one is
also kept (negative knowledge is knowledge). Detail → [PROTOCOL §3](../PROTOCOL.md).
